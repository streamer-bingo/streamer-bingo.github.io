Streamer Bingo

How To Play

  1. Enter all possible Bingo Card into the input fields
  2. (You can click the "+"-button if you wanna add more)
  3. 24 options are required as a minimum
  4. Once you press the "Generate" button, it will create a random board for you
  5. Left-clicking on any field will mark it as done, clicking again will unmark it
  6. A link to share with friends will be at the bottom
  7. Refreshing will always regenerate a new board
  8. Enjoy playing
